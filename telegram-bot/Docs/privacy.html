Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Telegram bot and connected services ("Service").

Information We Collect

  • Instagram Account Data: When you connect your Instagram account through our bot, we collect basic profile information via secure OAuth authentication (username, account ID, and profile picture URL).
  • Telegram User Data: We collect your Telegram user ID, username, and chat information to personalize your experience and provide services.
  • Automation Preferences: We store your selected hashtags, comment templates, follow/unfollow preferences, and other automation settings.
  • Analytics Data: We may collect aggregated data about your use of the Service to improve functionality and performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage your Instagram account securely through the Service.
  • To execute Instagram automation actions according to your settings (likes, comments, follows, etc.).
  • To provide personalized reports and notifications via Telegram.
  • To ensure service stability, security, and continuous improvements.

Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Your Instagram credentials are never stored; we only use access tokens securely obtained via OAuth.
  • All communications between you and our servers are encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS, SSL/TLS).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. Simply contact our support team via Telegram to request changes or account deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users via Telegram or through an announcement in the bot interface when changes are made.
